# Lesson 12: Layer 2s and the Future of DeFi

## ðŸŽ¯ Core Concept: Scaling Solutions

To solve Ethereum's scalability issues, the industry has adopted a "Rollup-Centric" roadmap. Layer 2 solutions process transactions off-chain and post proofs to Layer 1, reducing costs by 10-100x.

## ðŸ“š Types of Layer 2s

### Optimistic Rollups

Assume transactions are valid by default. Have a challenge period where fraud can be detected and reverted.

**Examples**: Arbitrum, Optimism

### ZK-Rollups

Use advanced cryptography to prove transaction validity instantly. No challenge period needed.

**Examples**: zkSync, Starknet
